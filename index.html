CALCULATOR

Calculator Instructions

Trigonometric Functions: Use sin(x), cos(x), tan(x), sin-1(x), cos-1(x), tan-1(x).

Logarithmic Functions: Use log(x) for log base 10 and ln(x) for natural log.

Mean: Use mean(x1,x2,...,xn) to calculate the mean of numbers.

Permutation (nPr): Use nPr(n,r) to calculate permutations.

Combination (nCr): Use nCr(n,r) to calculate combinations.

Square: Use a^2 to square a number.

Square Root: Use sqrt to find the square root.

Absolute Value: Use |a| to find the absolute value.

Round: Use round to round a number.

Floor: Use floor to get the largest integer less than or equal to a number.

Ceil: Use ceil to get the smallest integer greater than or equal to a number.
